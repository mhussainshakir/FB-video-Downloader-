Frequently asked questions

Is Glowdown free to use?

Yes. Pasting a link and downloading a video doesn't cost anything.

Can I download private or friends-only videos?

No. Glowdown can only read videos that are publicly visible on Facebook. Private, friends-only, or removed videos won't load.

What video quality can I get?

Glowdown shows every quality Facebook makes available for that video, usually including HD and SD options. Some older or short clips may only have one quality.

Why did fetching a video fail?

This usually means the link is private, the video was deleted, or the link format wasn't recognized. Open the video on Facebook, use Share → Copy link, and try the fresh link.

Is downloading Facebook videos allowed?

Facebook's own terms generally restrict downloading content you don't own. Only save videos you have the right to use — your own uploads, content shared under a license that allows it, or videos you have permission from the creator to keep. See the Terms section below for more.

Do you store the videos I download?

No. Glowdown asks Facebook's servers for a direct link and hands it to your browser — nothing is saved on our side.
